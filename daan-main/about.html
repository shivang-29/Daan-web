"Doing good feels good"

We are the students of Chandigarh University and we had a small step ahead for the society, As we work to create light for others, we naturally light our own way.

We are a non profit association which believes that India would have the option to accomplish more prominent heights by expanding schooling and that is the reason we have fabricated a group working productively in expanding education in rural area. With 4 individuals, working hard to plan and execute solutions for the issues faced by the children sleeping with an empty stomach. Each child should have the capability to understand their maximum potential through quality schooling to contribute towards an independent India. We will just leave you with one thought, You might be sitting on a couch with food prepared on the side-table however there are children lying on streets attempting to study with your tossed out old books.Let’s join hands together?
